Neuron Simulator

Synapse types

  • Green = excitatory (EPSP)
  • Red = inhibitory (IPSP)

Single synapse controls

  • Hover over a synapse to reveal controls
  • ➕ / ➖ adjust synaptic strength
  • Click a synapse to release neurotransmitter

Group firing

  • Hold Shift and click synapses to select them
  • Selected synapses are highlighted
  • Press Space to fire all selected synapses together

Use group firing to compare excitatory vs inhibitory influence.

What to observe

  • Small PSPs may decay before reaching the soma
  • Large excitatory PSPs can trigger an action potential
  • Inhibitory PSPs suppress depolarization
  • Multiple synapses can cooperate or compete
